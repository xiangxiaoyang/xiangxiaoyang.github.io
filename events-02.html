Simply Dan are an eleven-piece line-up of musi- cians who perform the music of Steely Dan- faithfully harnessing the jazz, pop and R&B influ- ences of the now legendary Walter Becker and Donald Fagen sound.

Made up of a 4 piece hom section, 2 guitars, 3 vocalists, keyboards and a bass and drums rhythm section, Simply Dan produce a wonderful tribute to the genius of Steely Dan-with the right balance between being respectful to the original recordings and firmly putting their own stamp on the material.
